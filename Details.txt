Divorce Control:
Description:
Divorce Control is an app where you can find the most appropriate reasons for divorce. This app describes how to control and avoid from divorce, how to enhance communication between a pair. 

Features:
•	Fully research based app on How to avoid from divorce.
•	The Research results on to enhance the best communication between pair.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	 Live Tiles(Locally & Remotely)
•	Searching feature.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing).
